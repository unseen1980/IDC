Emma Bull (born December 13, 1954) is an American science fiction and fantasy author.

 Her novels include the Hugo- and Nebula-nominated "Bone Dance" and the urban fantasy "War for the Oaks".

 She is also known for a series of anthologies set in Liavek, a shared universe that she created with her husband Will Shetterly.

 As a singer, songwriter, and guitarist, she has been a member of the Minneapolis-based folk/rock bands Cats Laughing and The Flash Girls.
